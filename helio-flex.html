PHOTOVOLTAIC CHARGING

Helio Flex embeds high-efficiency monocrystalline silicon cells across its flexible surface area. Photons excite electrons in the semiconductor lattice, generating direct current through the photovoltaic effect. The integrated MPPT controller optimizes power extraction across varying light conditions, from direct sunlight to diffuse indoor illumination.

Panel output scales with light intensity and angle of incidence. Built-in adaptive tracking algorithms maximize energy capture by dynamically adjusting the charging profile to match available irradiance, sustaining useful charging even under 5% full sun.

ELECTROMAGNETIC INDUCTION

The kinetic core uses Faraday's law of electromagnetic induction: a precision-engineered array of neodymium magnets oscillates through copper coil windings in response to mechanical movement. Any motion — walking, cycling, even the natural vibration of a bag — induces a fluctuating magnetic flux, driving alternating current in the coils.

An onboard full-wave bridge rectifier converts AC to regulated DC. The system operates across a wide frequency range (0.5–15 Hz), making it effective for low-frequency walking gaits as well as higher-frequency mechanical vibration, capturing useful power from virtually any kinetic source.
